## How to use this file

Log entries as you go. Be raw. Timestamp if you want, or just dump thoughts. The goal is to capture the *experience* — not produce a polished report.

Categories to track:
- **Intuitive** — things that clicked immediately
- **Opaque** — things that made no sense or had no explanation
- **Wrong** — things that felt like the wrong approach or wrong framing
- **Gave up** — moments you stopped trying and moved on
- **Questions** — things you wanted to ask but couldn't (per the rules)
- **Assumptions** — decisions you had to make with no guidance
